## What is Lounge Access?

Lounge access is a benefit offered on many Indian credit and debit cards that provides complimentary or discounted entry to airport lounges. Airport lounges offer comfortable seating, free food and beverages, Wi-Fi, showers, and a quiet environment away from crowded terminal areas.

## How Card-Based Lounge Access Works

Most cards provide lounge access through two primary programmes:

- **Visa Airport Companion (formerly DragonPass)**: Visa network cards
- **Mastercard Airport Experiences**: Mastercard network cards
- **DreamFolks**: Used by several Indian banks as the access platform

To use the benefit, you typically need to:
1. Show your eligible card at the lounge reception
2. Present a valid boarding pass
3. The visit is recorded against your card's quarterly/annual quota

Some banks now require activation through their app or a dedicated lounge access app before your visit.

## Domestic vs. International Lounge Access

| Feature | Domestic | International |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Available on | Most mid-range and premium cards | Premium and super-premium cards only |
| Visits per year | 2 – 32 | 2 – 16 |
| Typical value per visit | ₹1,200 – ₹1,800 | ₹2,500 – ₹5,000 |
| Guest access | Sometimes (at extra cost) | Rarely complimentary |

## Lounge Access by Card Tier

### Entry-Level Cards (2–4 visits/year)
- HDFC Millennia: 2 domestic visits per quarter
- IDFC First Select: 4 domestic visits per quarter

### Mid-Range Cards (8–16 visits/year)
- HDFC Regalia: 12 domestic + 6 international per year
- SBI Elite: 8 domestic + 2 international per year
- Axis My Zone: 8 domestic visits per year

### Premium Cards (Unlimited or 24+ visits/year)
- HDFC Infinia: Unlimited domestic and international
- Amex Platinum: Priority Pass with unlimited visits
- Axis Magnus: 24 domestic + 6 international per year

## Calculating the Value of Lounge Access

If you travel frequently, lounge access can be extremely valuable:

> Annual Value = Number of visits × Average value per visit

For example, HDFC Regalia's 18 annual visits at an average value of ₹1,500 each = **₹27,000 in annual lounge value**.

## Common Restrictions

- **Visit caps**: Most cards limit visits per quarter or year
- **Guest charges**: Bringing a guest may cost ₹1,200–₹2,500 per visit
- **Minimum gap**: Some cards require 2–3 hours between lounge visits at the same airport
- **Activation required**: Some banks require pre-registration in their app
- **Terminal restrictions**: Lounges are only available in select terminals
